Note: The Great War was not a trivial matter. In addition to taking the lives of roughly 40 million people, it cast an ominous shadow over the modern world for years and decades to come. It challanged the idea that modern people had become morally superior to our ancestors, and it set the stage for another sickening atrocity of the 20th Century, World War 2. Let us mourn and remember.
